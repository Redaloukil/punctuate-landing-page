For people with ADHD, hyperactivity and people who have a difficulty to focus on one tasks, Punctuate is a powerful web extension designed to help you stay present and maintain productivity while browsing the web.

Cut your work time by 50% by increasing your productivity and removing web distractions

According to Career builder, internet browsing is one of the biggest workplace distractions, by claiming back the time spent on the internet you are reducing the time spent on internet and creating more free time for your self

Try for free

Claim back your productivity and focus with these features

  • Dedicated modes

    On the same browser, configure your sessions whether for work time, for free time, for studying or research

  • Block distraction websites

    Disallow any temptations to go to websites that distract you from working or finishing your job

  • Set a tab limit

    Too many tabs on the browser are proven to be a distraction, limit your tab number and see the difference

  • Store session tabs

    Do not be afraid to close all your tabs when finishing work, we store them for you in the mode storage

  • Your privacy is important for us

    We do not store any of your data, all the sessions are kept on your browser storage
